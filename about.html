Our Story

Forged on the playground. Built for the league.

HoopZone Roots

Forging a Premium Standard

HoopZone was founded in 2018 in Brooklyn, New York, by a collective of high school basketball coaches and streetball organizers who grew tired of standard, commercialized sports gear that wore out after a single season of outdoor pick-up play.

Our mission is simple: to create professional-grade equipment, footwork conditioning gear, and court accessories that bridge the gap between amateur play and elite pro culture. We believe that every athlete, regardless of age or resource levels, deserves the feedback of high-end equipment.

"We don't build toys. We build training tools that translate directly to statistics in the game sheet."
Outreach & Impact

Basketball Community Focus

A portion of every purchase made at HoopZone goes directly towards sponsoring local public parks, funding summer tournaments, and donating professional backboards, rim nets, and balls to youth schools and academies in underserved neighborhoods.

Through our **Zone Initiative**, we have renovated 42 public concrete courts across the East Coast, providing high-visibility markings and steel double-loop chain nets that keep the basketball dream alive for thousands of kids daily.
